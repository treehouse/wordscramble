How To Play

This game will put your brain to the test! A random word will appear on the screen all jumbled up. You have seconds to figure out what the word is. Get it right and you'll instantly move on to the next word and increase your streak, get it wrong and you'll lose and have to start over. How high can you get your streak before you fail?
